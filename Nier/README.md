## A warped young woman with an overwhelming desire for love and acceptance arrives in Ylva.
A unique NPC to accompany you in your travels with her own feats, weapon, and spells. You can find her wandering the Lumiest Graveyard.
She is unlike other Unique NPCs though, in that you cannot recruit her through the usual methods, and instead
is simply recruited by talking and listening to her.

She is quite the strong ally. Use her at your own risk.
No, she does not have Death Reversed.
No, her story is not happy.

This mod is only in English, sorry.

Nier the character and the assets used in this mod are from the game Granblue Fantasy by Cygames.

Please report any bugs here or on the Elin Discord.

Requires [Custom Whatever Loader](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3370512305)
