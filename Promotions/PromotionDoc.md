# The Promotion Mod

Basically the player guide for the Promotion Mod

# Promotion Basics

To get the Promotion Manual, you'll need to talk to Lailah in the Quiet Cottage.  
Bring her 100 points worth of either Deciphered Ancient Books (2pts each) or Kumiromi Secrets (50pts each.)  
After turning them in, wait 3 days, and she will finish her work and be able to sell you the Promotion Manual.  
You will get your first Promotion Manual for free. Further manuals will cost 500k each.
Reach Level 20, and then read the Promotion Manual to select your choice of promotion.

# The Promotion Jobs and their Features.

These are organized by original Job. There are only promotions for player selectable vanilla Jobs.  
This unfortunately leaves Predator/Bard/Alchemist/Defender unable to promote.

# Warrior
## Sentinel - The Tank
The Sentinel is a defensive Warrior promotion that stands at the front lines. They are able to force enemies to target them instead of their allies, using their own superior defenses to reduce the damage.
Sentinels possess both offensive and defensive stances, changing between them as the situation calls for it.  

### Skills  
Shout: Taunts nearby enemies, causing them to prioritize targetting you.  

Shield Smite: Smashes the target with your shield, dealing damage and attempting to stun the target.  

Restraint Stance: Boosts your PV and Magic Resistance at the cost of your damage.

Rage Stance: Removes all your PV at the cost of increasing your melee damage.

### Passives
Sentinel: Increases your PV by 50% when using Heavy Armor. Increases your PV by 50% when using a Shield. These can stack.  

Block: When equipped with a Shield, you have a chance to block up to 85% of incoming melee damage. The % are based off your shield skill.

## Berserker - A cornered beast.
The Berserker is an offensive Warrior promotion that flourishes at the front lines. They grow stronger both defensively and offensively the more damage they take, living life on the edge to maximize their damage output.
Berserkers abilities cost life instead of mana or stamina to use.

### Skills
Bloodlust: Applies a Condition where you are silenced, but you will rapidly regenerate % HP every turn and will counterattack when hit with melee attacks.  

Sunder: Steals % HP from a target while swapping your debuffs with theirs.  

Lifebreak: Deals a heavy blow based on how much HP you are missing.

### Passives  
Berserk: Gains buffs when they are missing HP or afflicted with debuffs.  
- For every 5% missing HP or 1 Debuff on the Berserker, they will gain a 2% boost to all attributes.  
- When missing at least 25% HP, they will gain 10% overall Damage Reduction.  
- When missing at least 50% HP, they will increase their melee attack damage and accuracy.  
- When missing at least 75% HP, they will gain a speed boost.  

Revel: The Berserker will heal 25% of their maximum HP when they kill a target.

# Thief
## Hermit - The Assassin
The Hermit is an offensive Thief promotion that works from the shadows to assassinate targets. They are able to conceal themselves from view, waiting for the opportune moment to strike and eliminate their target.

### Skills
Mark for Death: Marks a target for assassination. Remaining close to the target will increase your stalking power.
Shadow Shroud: Conceals yourself with greater invisibilty. While hidden, your physical attacks will deal an additional 25% damage.
Assassinate: Inflicts a grievous wound against a target that is marked for death. This attack cannot miss, be parried, or countered.  
- If you have at least 20 stalk on the target, your hit will deal an additional 25% damage.
- If you have at least 30 stalk on the target, your hit will deal 2x damage and be a guaranteed critical hit.

### Passives 
Opportunist: Your attacks will be guaranteed critical hits with an additional 10% damage multiplier against targets that are asleep, paralyzed, or fainted.

## Trickster - Chaos Enjoyer
The Trickster is a support-type Thief promotion that controls the battlefield through mental attacks. They are able to lay Arcane Traps that will inflict mental debuffs on their enemies when triggered, as well as create illusions to distract and disrupt the enemy.
Tricksters have their own suite of mental debuffs they can apply to their foes.

### Skills
Arcane Trap: Set an arcane trap onto the ground that is triggerable by enemies. When triggered, they will explode in a small area, doing no damage, but afflicting a debuff.

Detonate Trap: Detonates the target arcane trap, causing it to explode.

Diversion: Creates three phantom trickster minions. They are incredibly fragile, but will inflict a Trickster debuff on their killer when they die.

Reversal: Targets an enemy and removes all debuffs on them. Protection will be applied to the Trickster's party based on how many debuffs were removed. Protection will absorb flat damage done to the target prior to damage reductions.

### Passives 
Elusive Trickster: When dodging an attack, the Trickster will create a phantom trickster minion.

# Wizard
## Elementalist
The Elementalist is an offensive Wizard promotion that unleashes an unending barrage of magical spells. They use elemental spells of all kinds to build up momentum, blowing away their opponents with sheer firepower.

### Skills
Elemental Fury: Close range elemental ultimate spell. Summons an elemental storm that follows the caster, consuming elemental orbs to strike nearby enemies with elemental swords.

Elemental Extinction: Wide range elemental ultimate spell. Every elemental orb consumed will drop a meteor of that element onto the closest target enemy.

### Passives
Elemental Stockpile: When elementalists cast attack spells, they will gain orbs of that element and stockpile them. Gaining a new type elemental orb will grant a stack of Spell Tempo as well as reduce the target's resistance to that element. You can only have 3 orbs of each element. When you haven't gained elemental orbs for 5 turns, you will lose a random orb every turn.

Spell Tempo: Elementalists will gain speed, mana regeneration, and spell enhance for every stack of Spell Tempo they have.

Elemental Mastery: Elementalists have double the Elemental Power, improving their ability to apply statuses.

Aspect of Torrents: When the Elementalists summon funnels, they will summon multiple at once that will start with Boost and Protection.

Arsenal Conspectus: When reading spellbooks for Elemental Attack Spells, Elementalists will also gain charges of two other elements in the attack spell (For example, reading Fire Arrow might grant charges of Cold and Ether Arrow as well.)

## Necromancer
The Necromancer is a support-type Wizard promotion that calls for the legion of the undead. They can summon legions of the undead to overwhelm their opponents.

### Skills
Beckon of the Dead: Marks an enemy. If a target dies while under the effect of Beckon of the Dead, they will be revived as a skeletal minion and added to the Necromancer's ranks.

Blessing of the Dead: Sacrifices an undead minion to heal nearby allies based on how much HP they had left. Overhealing will be converted into Protection.

Corpse Explosion: Sacrifies an undead minion to cause a nether explosion at their position.

Summon Skeleton: Necromancer's signature spell. Summons a skeleton minion for the Necromancer.
- Skeleton Warriors
- Skeleton Mages
- Death Knights

### Passives 
Necromancy Conspectus: Necromancers can convert other summoning spells into Summon Skeleton.

Bone Armor: For every undead minion the Necromancer controls, they have 2.5% combat damage reduction. This effect caps at 75% damage reduction.

# Farmer
## Jenei
The Jenei is an offensive spellcaster Farmer promotion that specializes in the four natural elements. They can gather spirits of Earth, Fire, Wind, and Water to aid them, combining their powers to summon great representatives of the natural forces to aid them in combat.
Jenei are aligned with one of the four primal elements, gaining access to additional abilities unique to their element.

### Skills
Spirit Summon: The main power of the Jenei. By using all of their elemental spirits, they can use the power of a Grand Spirit. Grand Spirits have a variety of effects, both offensive and defensive. Offensive Grand Spirits do scaled damage based on the Maximum HP of the target.

#### Venus
Move: Pushes the target object one tile if possible. This can be used on installed objects.

Mother Gaia: Causes an earthen spire to erupt at the target location, dealing localized impact damage.
#### Mars
Blaze: Does fire damage to a tile, setting the tile ablaze.

Dragon Fume: Breathes dragonfire in the target direction, causing severe burns.
#### Jupiter
Reveal: Reveals the secrets of the target, allowing you to see their character sheet. When used against enemies, this will also inflict Magic Break, which reduces their magic resistance. When used against neutral targets, it will reveal their favored items.

Shine Plasma: Drops a lightning bolt on the target.
#### Mercury
Deluge: Drops a deluge of water on the target, guaranteed to inflict wet and leave a puddle.

Ply: Heals the target for a large amount (Equivalent to Heal spell)

### Passives 
Adept Conspectus: Jenei can convert elemental attack spellbooks into the four elements they prefer, Impact, Fire, Lightning, and Ice.

## Druid
The Druid is a support and summoning type Farmer promotion that brings forth nature's vitality to the battlefield. They can summon various kinds of plantlife to strengthen their allies or weaken their enemies.

### Skills
Sow Wrath Seeds: Summons a stationary invulnerable minion that spread a variety of debuffs to enemies around them.

Sow Warmth Seeds: Summons a stationary invulnerable minion that spreads a variety of buffs to allies around them.

Living Armor: Wraps an ally with living vines, increasing PV and healing them over time.

Summon Tree Ent Warrior: Summons a tree ent warrior to assist you in combat. Has a small chance of summoning an aspect of nature.

### Passives 
Nature Conspectus: Druids can convert other summoning spells into Summon Tree Ent Warrior.

Friend of Nature: Wild Plants and Animal life are friendly to you.

# Archer
## Sniper
The Sniper is an offensive Archer promotion that flourishes at the back lines. They are able to remove priority targets from the fight, either permanently through a vital point, or temporarily through crippling a body part.

### Skills
Target Head: Makes a ranged attack that can inflict silence and dim. This attack has a 25% chance to cull any enemies at or below 30% HP.

Target Hand: Makes a ranged attack that can disable melee and ranged attacks.

Target Legs: Makes a ranged attack that can slow the target.

Spread Shot: Makes ranged attacks against all targets in a cone.

### Passives
No Distractions: When there are no enemies within 3 tiles of you, you will have increased crit, accuracy, and rapid fire.

## Ranger
The Ranger is a control-type Archer promotion that rides through the battlefield. They use special munitions and traps to debuff enemies.
Rangers possess a unique stance: Ranger's Canto, which allows them to fire even while moving as long as they are mounted.

### Skills
Ranger's Canto: A stance that allows the Ranger to automatically make ranged attacks against nearby hostiles when moving while mounted.

Gimmick Shot: Loads up one of 5 Gimmick shots, causing your next ranged attacks to apply specific statuses.
- Shatter Shot: Projectiless will split off and target two additional hostiles.
- Hammer Shot: Projectiles can apply dim.
- Serrated Shot: Projectiles can apply bleed.
- Venom Shot: Projectiles can apply poison.
- Paralytic Shots: Projectiles can apply paralysis.

Throw Trap: Lays a random trap in the target location, triggerable by enemies.
- Blast Mine: A landmine that explodes, dealing damage, applying dim, and knocking enemies away.
- Paralytic Mine: A landmine that explodes and spreads paralytic gasses, paralyzing enemies.
- Poison Gas Mine:: A landmine that explodes and spreads poisonous gasses, poisoning enemies.
- Punji Trap: A trap that deals damage, inflicts bleed and slow to targets
- Ranger Snare: A trap that entangles targets.

### Passives 
None.

Battlemages possess two unique different stances. Both stances will reserve a portion of the mana to boost either their defensive or offensive capabilities. These stances can be used at the same time.

They dominate the battlefield with relentless offensive capabilities whilst they walk the thin line between life and death, using their own health to empower their spells.

### Skills

The Tourist is a unique Job in that they are able to choose Promotion Jobs from other Jobs.

## Adventurer
The Adventurer is a PC only support-type Tourist promotion that is focused on exploration. While they lack the offensive benefits of other classes, they make up for it with their unique exploration based abilities and passives, leading the way into nefias, and out with heavier pockets.

### Skills
This Way: Only usable in nefia. Teleports the player to the entrance stairs, or as close to it.

# Pianist
## Dancer
The Dancer is a support-type Pianist promotion that spreads buffs and debuffs across the battlefield with their exotic dances. They possess a multitude of mystical dances in their repetoire, as well as additional offensive flourishes that can be used during their dances.
Dancers can also choose to support a single ally with the Partner Stance, focusing the buffs on their single ally and themselves.

### Skills
Dance Partner: Designates a party member as a dance partner. Your dances will instead apply at 150% power only to you and your dance partner.

#### Dances: The dancer can only perform once dance at a time as a stance.
Energizing Tango: A dance that reduces stamina and mana usage by 15%.

Liberating Foxtrot: A dance that will remove a debuff each turn.

Soothing Samba: A dance that heals every turn.

Obscuring Kagura: A dance that increases the PV and DV.

Swift Salsa: A dance that increases speed.

#### Flourishes: The Dancer can perform flourish attacks while dancing. Dance Partners will automatically perform follow up attacks if they are in melee range of the targets.

Dagger Illusion: Fling throwing weapons at up to 5 nearby targets mid dance, inflicting statuses. Can only be used if you have a throwing weapon.

Sword Fouette: A sword dance that will perform a melee attack against all nearby enemies within 2 tiles.

Wild Pirouette: An enchanting confusing dance that inflicts debuffs against nearby enemies.

### Passives
None

## Knightcaller
The Knightcaller is a summoner-type Pianist promotion that commands a ghostly Knight Order. Knights are powerful summons that, while few in number, are exeedingly strong in their own right. The Knightcaller can summon a variety of Knights to the fight, as well as Knight Commanders.
Whenever the Knightcaller summons a knight, they will suffer from summoning sickness, which will temporarily reduce their maximum mana for a short time.

### Skills
Enrage Spirits: Enrages your minions, buffing their offenses.

Rally Spirits: Recalls all minions within 20 tiles to your side, healing them and buffing their defenses.

Mobilize Spirits: Teleports all minions within 10 tiles to the target location.

Summon Knight: Summons a Spirit Knight minion to fight for you. If you do not have an active Captain, there is a 20% chance to summon a Spirit Knight Captain. Adds a stack of summoning sickness.
- Spirit Knights are incredibly powerful minions. There are 7 different kinds of Spirit Knights that can be summoned, as well as 7 different Spirit Knight Captains.

### Passives 
None

# Priest
## Saint
The Saint is a spell focused Priest promotion that provides both healing and Holy spell support. They are able to strengthen the connection between their allies and their gods with their unique buff, while providing advanced healing support.
Saints can convert other elemental attack spellbooks into the Holy element.

### Skills
Hand of God: A large scale healing spell that heals all friendlies nearby for 35% of their maximum HP and applies a greater healing over time effect. 30 Turn Cooldown.

Invigorate: Buffs the target with temporarily increased Max HP.

Blessing: Buffs the target with temporarily increased Faith.

### Passives
God Protects: When praying for the first time a day, Saints will also apply Protection to all allies.

In their Name: When fighting an enemy that follows the same religion, the Saint can convert them into a minion if they have a higher Faith skill level.

Light Conspectus: Saints can convert elemental attack spellbooks into the Holy element.

## War Cleric
The War Cleric is an defensive Priest promotion that is welcome on any front line. They can save allies from dangerous situations, capable of protecting, healing, or harming as needed.

### Skills
Divine Descent: A powerful skill that invokes godly powers. Deals holy damage around you while inflicting fear, heals allies to full HP and purges any debuffs on them. Massively boosts your own attributes for the duration of the skill. Only usable once a day.

Deploy Sanctuary: A skill that creates a protective field around you. Any character within the field (enemy or ally) will take 75% reduced damage.

Divine Fist: Strike a target with a holy fist that creates four holy bolts on hit, which will pick out the closest 4 targets and fly at them. Enemies hit will take holy damage while allies will be healed.

### Passives 
Benevolence: Any healing applied to the War Cleric themselves will be applied at 50% power to all friendly characters within 3 tiles.

God Protects: When praying for the first time a day, War cleric will also apply Protection to all allies.

# Gunner
## Sharpshooter
The Sharpshooter is a defensive Gunner promotion which flourish on the backlines. They provide deadly accurate suppressive fire and target marking for their team.
Sharpshooters possess the Overwatch Stance, which will reduce their speed, but allows them to establish a killzone. Any enemy that moves within this killzone will cause the Sharpshooter to open fire.

### Skills
Overwatch Stance: A stance that lowers your speed by 15%. In exchange, it increases your hit chance, critical chance, critical range, vision range. Any enemies within your field of vision will have Overwatched applied to them and you will automatically fire your ranged weapon at them whenever they move.

Charged Shot: A buff that consumes a percent of your mana to add it as flat damage to your next ranged attack.

Mark Hostiles: Marks all enemies in an area, reducing their DV. Every target you mark will restore 5% of your MP.

### Passives 
Suppressive Fire: Ranged attacks will always apply Suppression against the targets.

## Machinist
The Machinist is a summoner-type Gunner promotion which brings overwhelming firepower to the battlefield. Specializing in various kinds of firearms, they are able to load homemade munitions into their firearms and autoturrets.
Machinists can convert other summoning-type spellbooks into their own spell, the Summon Turret spell.

### Skills
Heavyarms Stance: Enters the Heavyarms stance, which stops you from moving and applies gravity to yourself. However, in this stance you will gain a 25% Speed Increase, 10% damage reduction, have increased fire rate (Rapid Arrow 2), any follow up any ranged attack with a missile salvo. In this stance your cooldowns will expire twice as fast.

Load Up: Reloads your equipped gun with custom munitions. Also applies to nearby machine allies.
- Armor Piercing Rounds: These bullets have Vorpal and Drill.
- Incendiary Rounds: These bullets inflict fire damage and have scatter.
- Tracer Rounds: these bullets have increased accuracy and chase the targets.

Overclock: Buffs yourself and all nearby Machine-type allies with Overclock, increasing speed and fire rate (Rapid Arrow 1). 

Summon Turret: A spell that summons an autonomous turret minion armed with a gun. 

### Passives
Machinery Conspectus: Machinists canconvert other summoning spells into Summon Turret.

# Inquisitor
## Witch Hunter
The Witch Hunter is an anti-magic focused Inquisitor promotion. They possess an array of abilities at their disposal that grants them heavy advantage over any spellcaster, such as silencing or targetting mana as well as life.
Witch Hunters can deploy a Null Zone around them which massively hampers any sort of spellcasting.

### Skills
Mana Break: Causes a magical explosion on the target that scales based on how much mana they are missing.

Magic Reflect: Buffs yourself with a reflective shield that redirects a set number of incoming magical spells towards the caster.

Null Zone: You emit a null zone in a 5 tile radius. In this Null Zone, spells will have 0% cast chance.

### Passives 
Bane of Magickind: Bane will additionally inflict Silence.

Rend Mana: Whenever you deal combat damage, 10% of the damage will also be dealt to their Mana.

## Justicar
The Justicar is an anti-physical focused Inquisitor promotion. They are able to intimidate and control groups of enemies through their debuffs, reducing their physical abilities.

### Skills
Intimidate: Inflicts Armor Break, reducing the PV of the target. Inflicts Fear on other enemies around the target.

Subdue: Inflicts Suppress and Attack Break, reducing the Attack of the target.

Condemn: Inflicts Entangle in the area. Every enemy impacted will apply Protection to allies.

Flames of Judgement: Surrounds yourself with magical flames, dealing a % of your maximum life as fire damage to yourself as well as nearby enemies every turn.
- If the Justicar is Good aligned (0 or Greater Karma as the Player. Party Members will use the PC's Karma), this will also heal allies for 1/2 the damage that would have been dealt.
- If the Justicar is Evil aligned (Less than 0 Karma as the Player. Party Members will use the PC's Karma), this will also inflict Fire Break on any enemies, reducing their fire resistances.

### Passives 
None

# Paladin
## Sovereign
The Sovereign is a support Paladin promotion that oversees the battlefield with their tactics. Sovereigns command multiple buffing abilities that each have two different effects. By changing between Law and Chaos March, they can tune their buffs to be more offensive or defensive in nature.

### Skills
Law March: Begin the Law March, granting you and your allies a 5% damage reduction, while causing your Battle Orders to be defensive in nature. Cannot be used at the same time as Chaos March.

Chaos March: Begin the Chaos March, granting you and your allies a 5% damage increase, while causing your Battle Orders to be offensive in nature. Cannot be used at the same time as Law March.

#### Battle Orders - Sovereigns have 3 different Battle Orders, each with two effects depending on whether they are in Law or Chaos mode. Activating an order of the opposite spectrum will cancel the original if still active.
Formation Order
- Law: Grants allies Barricade Formation, which reduces the damage they take by 5% for each neighboring ally with Barricade Formation.
- Chaos: Grants allies Sword Formation, which allows any neighboring allies in Sword Formation to make a followup melee or ranged attack. They can only make one followup attack a turn.

Morale Order
- Law: Activates Victory March, Increases all attributes, maximum life and mana.
- Chaos: Activates Til Death, granting allies increased speed and combat skills.

Strategy Order:
- Law: Activates Rally Order, which heals nearby allies, grants them protection, as well as life and mana regeneration.
- Chaos: Activates Rout Order, which grants allies Holy Intonation as well as providing life, mana, and stamina on kill.

### Passives
None

## Holy Knight
The Holy Knight is a defensive Paladin promotion that rushes the enemy with a host of Holy attacks. Boasting impressive survivability, they utilize their skills to protect and sustain themselves, as well as create Holy Sword bits to assist them in combat.

### Skills
Vanguard Stance: Activates a stance that redirects all damage that would be done to nearby non-summon allies to you.

Spearhead Charge: Charges to a specific point. Can go through enemies, knocking them away. Every enemy you charge through will summon a Holy Sword Funnel minion.

Deflection: Enter Deflection state for a single turn. Taking damage while in this state will reduce the damage by 100%, reduce the cooldown, and summon a Holy Sword Funnel minion.
Plant Holy Banner: Summon and plant an invulnerable Holy Banner minion on the target tile. While it does not move or attack, it will apply effects to characters within 3 tiles.
- Any enemies will be hit with Holy Damage that can stun them. This power is doubled if the target is an Undead or a Demon.
- Any allies will be granted Protection.

### Passives 
Heavenly Host: Whenever you summon a Holy Sword Funnel minion, you will gain a stack of Heavenly Host. Each stack of Heavenly Host grants 2% damage reduction, capping at 20% with 10 Stacks.

# Witch
## Hexer
The Hexer is a debuff oriented Witch promotion that capitalizes on applying a variety of ailments. Capable of hexing their enemies with both physical and mental conditions, Hexers pile on the ailments, becoming more and more dangerous as they stack up.

### Skills
Blood Curse: Uses 10% of your life to apply a curse to the target.

Revenge: Consumes a debuff on yourself and uses the power to deal mind damage against the target.

Traumatize: Deals heavy mind damage against a target that scales with how many negative conditions that are on the target. Costs 20% of your maximum mana to use. Does not consume the negative conditions.

### Passives
Hexmaster: When applying spell damage or taking damage, you have a chance to apply a hex to the target or the aggressor. 

Hex Maniac: When you are afflicted with a debuff, your Debuff power is increased by 50%.

## Artificer
The Artificer is a creation based Witch promotion that creates all kinds of magical devices for combat. The tools produced by an Artificer have a wide variety of effects for both friend and foe. Artificers can also produce their own golem ally, a heavily customizable partner that can come in various configurations.


### Skills
Simple Synthesis: Reloads Artificer Tools and any guns in your party. Ammo reloaded is Ether Ammo based off your current spell power.

Improvised Brew: Mixes and throws a random potion at a target. It will throw either a positive or negative effect potion based on whether the target is friendly or hostile.
### Passives 
Material Hunter: When Sun/Earth/Mana crystals are mined, an extra one will be dropped.

Artificer: Artificers gain access to the Artificer's Altar. Here they can craft a variety of magical tools for both offensive and defensive purposes. They also can craft the Artificer Golem, which is a customizeable one of a kind powerful ally.

# Executioner
## Headhunter
The Headhunter is an offensive Executioner promotion that thrives on momentum. They grow stronger with kills, gaining temporary buffs with each enemy slain in a single battle. They are also capable of executing low health enemies to further increase their own power.

### Skills
Execute: A strong melee attack that has a 100% chance to cull any target at or below 25% HP. If the cull was successful, grants the Momentum buff to you and your allies, which increases PV and Speed.

Reap: A cleaving melee attack that does does increased damage against full health enemies or enemies with Bad conditions. Also inflicts Armor Break to reduce their PV.

### Passives 
Trophy Hunter: Gain a damage multiplier when attacking rare enemies. When you slay an enemy with active buffs, a random buff is taken and applied to you.

Headhunting: When landing the killing blow on any enemy, you gain a stack of Headhunter.
- Each Headhunter stack reduces the cost of your abilities, capping at 100% reduction at 10 Stacks.
- Each Headhunter stack increases your damage dealt by 3% and decreases your damage taken by 3%, capping at 30% of each at 10 stacks.

## Harbinger
The Harbinger is a debuff oriented Executioner promotion that focuses on spreading miasma across the battlefield. Capable of applying a variety of miasma effects, they grow stronger as their enemies are choking under the relentess assault of miasma.

### Skills
Endless Mists: Extends the debuff duration of all Harbinger Miasmas on nearby enemies.

Accursed Touch: Applies a Harbinger Miasma to the target.
- Harbinger Miasmas differ from normal Miasmas as they CAN stack with each other.

Gloom: Reserves a portion of your mana to emit a cloud of miasma around you in a radius of 3 tiles. Enemies exposed to this cloud will have a random Harbinger Miasma applied every turn.

### Passives 
Hunter in the Mists: Harbingers deal an additional 5% damage to the target for every active Miasma they suffer from.

Miasmic Armor: Every time a new Harbinger Miasma is applied to any nearby enemy, the Harbinger will gain a stack of Miasma Armor, granting 2% damage reduction per stack and capping at 19 stacks.

Deep Breather: If any of the Harbinger's allies are afflicted with a Miasma (either normal or Harbinger), the Harbinger will absorb it instead, healing for 10% health.

Smoke Conspectus: Harbingers can convert elemental attack spells into Miasma spells of the same element.

# Swordsage
## Rune Knight
The Runeknight is a defensive Sword Sage promotion that focuses on protecting against elements. They are able to use their special Runic Guard ability to attune themselves to an element, gaining completely immunity from that element. The more they absorb of that element, the more powerful their retaliation will be.

### Skills
Runic Guard: Adopt a defensive stance that allows you to absorb and attune to the next elemental attack you take.
- When you attune to an element, you will gain the Elemental Attunement condition, which will protect you from incoming damage of the specified element, absorbing the damage and recovering mana based on the damage of the attack. When not absorbing damage, the stored damage will decay by 5% a turn.

Spinning Slash: Requires you to have attuned to an Element. The stored elemental damage is consumed and used to fuel a powerful spinning slash, striking all enemies within a 3 tile radius with an elemental sword made from your attunement. Afterwards, you will lose the Elemental Attunement condition.

Rune Etching: Applies a protective talisman with a set amount of charges to the target. When taking damage, these talismans will activate and reduce the damage taken, as well as knock back the aggressor. When there is an incoming debuff, this talisman will also activate and negate the debuff.

### Passives 
None

## Spellblade
The Spellblade is a debuff oriented Sword Sage promotion that cripples the enemy with precise melee strikes. They can do physical disables by executing crushing strikes, or reduce the enemy's elemental resistance with their enchanted swords.
When Spellblades summon elemental funnels, they will summon melee oriented Sword Funnels instead.

### Skills
Crushing Strike: Targets a random body part of the enemy that will apply a different debuff based on which body part it hits.

Myriad Fleche: Lunge at the enemy with a thrusting melee attack. On impact, release a wave of elemental damage in a cone, striking targets behind the enemy you lunged at and weakening their resistance to that element. This wave's element is based on your current active Intonation. If you have no Intonation active, a random intonation from Fire, Lightning, Cold, and Poison is chosen and applied to you.

Siphoning Blade: Strikes a target with a melee attack that absorbs half the damage done as mana.

### Passives 
Blade Conspectus: Spellblades can convert elemental attack spells into either Intonation or Magic Sword of the same element.

Elemental Mastery: Spellblades have double the Elemental Power, improving their ability to apply statuses.
